Research Interests:

Gesture and sign language research, bilingualism and second language acquisition, sentence processing and literacy.

Recent Publications:

Piñar, P, Dussias, P. E., and Morford, J. P. (2011). Deaf readers as bilinguals: An examination of deaf readers’ print comprehension in light of current advances on bilingualism and second language processing. Language and Linguistics Compass, 5(10), 691-704.

Taub, S., Galvan, D., & Piñar, P. (2009). The role of gesture in crossmodal typological studies. Cognitive Linguistics, 20(1), 71-92.

Taub, S., Galvan, D.  Piñar, P. & Mather, S. (2008). Gesture and ASL L2 Acquisition. In Muller de Quadros, R. (ed.). Theoretical Issues in Sign Language Research, 9, 639-651.

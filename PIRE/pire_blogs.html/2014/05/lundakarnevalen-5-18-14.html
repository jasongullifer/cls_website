Lundakarnevalen 5-18-14

By JULIA ROSE HOTCHNER on May 18, 2014 11:52 AM | No Comments | No TrackBacks
Hello all!

Today marks the end of the "Lundakarnevalen" or the Lund carnival. Every four years Lund  university holds a three day celebration. It includes parades, concerts and food from all over . The student body puts a lot of time into organizing this weekend. My roommates and I attended the parade on Saturday. It was a sunny day filled with marching bands floats and lots of excitement .

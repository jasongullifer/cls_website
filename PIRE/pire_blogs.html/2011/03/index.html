PIRE Partners in the News: VL2 and Memory and Language Research Group

By Jason on March 31, 2011 9:21 AM | No Comments | No TrackBacks
Two recent press releases highlight the collaborations with PIRE partners. One is the recent TV show featuring Teresa Bajo's lab. The segment, linked here, is all in Spanish and the work by Teresa's group starts at around 9:30--you'll definitely see some friendly faces. If you head to around 13:00 and you'll see our very own visual world experiment being demonstrated and pay close attention and you'll hear Rosa Guzzardo's voice--recorded for life on this news segment!

The other segment, linked here, is on the work with Jill Morford and VL2. This was issued as a press release from Penn State but has been picked up in many places, including US News and World Reports.

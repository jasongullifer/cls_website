What does it mean to become a PIRE undergraduate student?

2015 PIRE undergraduate students will conduct innovative research on bilingualism at leading collaborating institutions in Europe and Asia (click here to learn more about our internationals partners). PIRE students will receive an all-expenses-paid trip abroad during the summer of 2015 in order to work on a language science research project. To be eligible for selection as a PIRE student, you must have completed two semesters of research with a Center for Language Science faculty member.

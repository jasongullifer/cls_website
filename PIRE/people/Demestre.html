Foreign Partners - Dr. Josep Demestre

Position: Associate Professor at the Psychology Department

Institution: Universitat Rovira i Virgili, Spain

Email: josep.demestre@urv.cat

Lab/Group: Psycholinguistics Research Group

Bio:

Dr. Demestre completed his doctoral studies in the interdisciplinary program in Cognitive Science and Language at the Universitat de Barcelona, Universitat Aut—noma de Barcelona and Universitat Rovira i Virgili, with a specialization in language processing and parsing. He received his PhD in Cognitive Science at the Universitat Rovira i Virgili (Tarragona, Spain) in 2003.

Research Interest:

The focus of his research is human language processing, in particular the comprehension of sentences. He is also interested in bilingualism, focusing on the processing and representation of L1 and L2 words. He uses behavioral (e.g., reaction time measures and eye tracking methodology) and electrophysiological (ERPs) techniques to assess the cognitive and neural mechanisms involved in language processing at this level.

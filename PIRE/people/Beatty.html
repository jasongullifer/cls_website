Graduate Students - Anne Beatty-Martínez

Position: Graduate Student- Spanish Linguistics and Language Science

Institution: Pennsylvania State University, USA

Email: alb507@psu.edu

CV: View as PDF

Lab/Group:Department of Spanish, Italian, and Portuguese

Bio:

I am Ph.D. student in Spanish and Language Science working with Prof. Giuli Dussias at Penn State. My research agenda involves the integration of linguistic, cognitive and neuroscientific approaches to examine how the way bilinguals use their languages may shape their cognitive profiles. My ongoing research examines the extent to which distributional patterns found in naturally-occurring code-switching production predict comprehension performance. This summer I will work in Teresa Bajo's lab at the University of Granada where I will conduct an ERP study to examine predictive processing strategies in non-habitual code-switchers.

PIRE Projects: bilingualism, code-switching, prediction and working memory.

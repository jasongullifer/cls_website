Undergraduate Students - Kaylee Roupas

Position: Undergraduate Student

Institution: Pennsylvania State University, USA

Email: kma5296@psu.edu

Lab/Group:Bilingualism and Language Development Lab (BiLD), James McQueen

Bio: I am entering my senior year of my undergraduate studies, and am planning on graduating with a major in Linguistics (Bachelor of Philosophy) and minors in Classics & Ancient Mediterranean Studies, French & Francophone Studies, and International Studies. I have had the privilege to be a member of the CLS lab for the past two years; now I am beyond thrilled to have been given the opportunity to continue my research interests abroad with the PIRE program. I will be examining the influence of L1 (Dutch) notional concepts and grammatical gender on L2 (English) subject-verb agreement in Nijmegen, the Netherlands.
